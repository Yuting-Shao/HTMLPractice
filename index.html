The Nobel Prize 2022

Physics Prize 2022

Using goundbreaking experiments, Alain Aspect, John Clauser and Anton Zeilinger have demonstrated the potential to investigate and control particles that are in entangled states.

Chemistry Prize 2022

Barry Sharpless and Morten Meldal brought chemistry into the era of functionalism and laid the foundatiions of click chemistry.

Medicine Prize 2022

Svante Paabo discovered the genomes of extinct hominins and human evolution.

Literature Prize 2022

Annie Ernaux -- "for the courage and clinical acuity with which she uncovers the roots, estrangements and collective restraints of peronal memory".
